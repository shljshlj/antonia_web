About Me

Hello, my name is Antonia. I grew up on a small island and was strongly influenced by the sea. I received an M.A. in Maritime studies. Parallel to work in that field, I gained experience volunteering with kids.

I'm a passionate swimmer, cyclist, photographer and also an aspiring writer. I happily transfer my love for arts and sports to kids. I believe that every child has huge potential and I enjoy working with them to overcome challenges.

I have experience working with children with special needs, in particular with those on the Autism spectrum. By building up mutual respect, enforcing positive communication and with lots of hard work I have witnessed great transformations in children I have worked with; I like to think that my work contributed to those positive changes.
