About Me

Hello, my name is Antonia. I grew up on a small island and was strongly influenced by the sea. I received an M.A. in Maritime studies, I was sailing, but also in parallel was volunteering with kids. And slowly I was getting experience in that field.

I am a passionate swimmer and a cyclist, a photographer and a writer also. I am happily transfering my love for arts and sports to kids. I believe that every child has huge potential and I enjoy working with them to overcome challenges.

I have experience working with children with special needs, in particular with those on the Autism spectrum. Practicing respect, good communication and hard work I have witnessed great transformations in children I have worked with; I would like to think that my work was part of those positive changes.
